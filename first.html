About Our Institute

The Augustinian Institute, Makurdi is a premier institution dedicated to the pursuit of academic excellence, spiritual formation, and intellectual development. As an affiliate of Rev. Fr. Moses Orshio Adasu University, we uphold the highest standards of education rooted in Catholic traditions and Augustinian values.

Our mission is to form individuals who are not only academically competent but also morally upright, spiritually mature, and committed to serving humanity with wisdom and compassion.
