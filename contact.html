Find Us

From Makurdi City Center

Take the Otukpo Road heading east. The Institute is located approximately 5km from the city center, on the right side of the road.

Public Transportation

Take a bus or taxi heading to Otukpo and ask to be dropped at Augustinian Institute. Most drivers are familiar with the location.

Parking Information

Visitor parking is available at the main entrance. Please check in at the security post for a visitor's pass.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our administrative offices are open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and on Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. The library has extended hours: Monday to Friday 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Saturday 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM, and Sunday 12:00 PM to 4:00 PM.

You can schedule a campus tour by contacting the Admissions Office at +234 803 059 3538 or emailing admissions@aimakurdi.ng. Tours are typically scheduled on weekdays between 9:00 AM and 3:00 PM.

Transcript requests should be directed to the Academic Affairs office. You can email academicaffairs@aimakurdi.ng or call +234 702 123 4567. Please allow 5-7 working days for processing.

Yes, our campus is accessible to people with disabilities. We have ramps, elevators in multi-story buildings, and accessible restrooms. If you require specific accommodations during your visit, please contact us in advance so we can ensure your needs are met.

For technical support with the student portal, please contact the ICT Department at +234 805 678 9012 or email ict@aimakurdi.ng. Support is available during office hours, and emergency support may be available for critical issues.
